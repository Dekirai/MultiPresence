# Placeholders
|Placeholder|Function|Usable in|
|--|--|--|
|{hp}|Shows your current HP|Default|
|{maxhp}|Shows your current max HP|Default|
|{gameicon}|Shows the image of the current active game, to be used with Large-/Smallasset|Everywhere|
|{gamename}|Shows the name of the current active game|Everywhere|
